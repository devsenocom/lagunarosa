1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be processed when you visit lagunarosa.com (the “Website”), an informational site about Laguna Rosa, a free-to-play casual idle RPG with mature-oriented content.

The purpose of this policy is to describe what limited technical information may be processed when you use the Website and how it is used.

This Privacy Policy should be read together with the Website’s Terms of Service.

The Website does not intentionally collect or store personal data such as:

Visitors are not required to create accounts or provide personal information to browse the Website.

3. Technical Log Data

Like most websites, the server may automatically generate limited technical logs when the Website is accessed.

These logs may include:

This information is used solely for:

  • maintaining website security
  • diagnosing technical issues
  • generating aggregated usage statistics

Technical logs may be deleted or anonymized automatically after a limited retention period.

The Website may use minimal first-party cookies or similar technologies for basic website functionality.

These cookies may be used for purposes such as:

These technologies do not require you to create an account on the Website.

Users can disable cookies through their browser settings.

5. Use of Information

Any technical information generated by the Website may be used only for:
