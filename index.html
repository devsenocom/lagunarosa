About Game

Welcome aboard, Captain. Laguna Rosa is a free casual idle RPG, anime dating sim, and visual novel on a pirate island you call your own. The Rose Lagoon is a picturesque speck of land in the middle of the ocean — beautiful beaches hide debts, intrigue, and danger, but also people counting on you. Build and upgrade structures, assign workers, and turn ruins into a center of power and luxury. Win over a wide cast of heroines: chat, give gifts, and raise affection from flirting to full-fledged dates. The further you go, the more rewards you unlock — unique illustrations and animated adult scenes — while their talents help you outsmart enemies and reclaim what rival pirates stole.

Gameplay

The heiress of House Rose has fallen into a pirate gang's trap. Only you — a free pirate bearing the Sea Serpent's Mark — can change her fate: rebuild the port from ruins, gather a team of talented beauties, and take the island back from those who call it their prize. Unlock, repair, and upgrade buildings, grow the economy, and expand influence through diplomacy or dirty espionage. Set off on adventures to learn what past the Rose Lagoon hides, why the ocean is ringed by the Stormbelt, and why the Sea Serpent granted you power — all while your indebted backwater becomes a pirate capital of luxury.

FAQ

What is Laguna Rosa?

A free-to-play casual idle RPG on a pirate island: build and upgrade structures, romance heroines, and uncover the secrets of the Rose Lagoon. You play as a Captain with the Sea Serpent's Mark — rebuild the port, recruit beauties, and take the island back from rival pirates. Available in browser and on Android.

Is it free to play?

Yes. You can play in your browser or on Android without paying. Optional in-game purchases are available for faster progress or convenience.

What genre is Laguna Rosa?

Casual idler RPG, anime dating sim, and visual novel — an idle island-builder with story adventures, relationship routes, and mature rewards.

Where can I play?

In web browsers and on Android.

Which languages are supported?

English, Russian, Spanish, German, French, Italian, Portuguese, Polish, Turkish, Japanese, Korean, and Chinese (Simplified). The in-game list may change over time.

Is this game for adults only?

Yes. Laguna Rosa is aimed at mature audiences and includes adult-oriented romance, illustrations, and animated scenes. Some regions require players to be 18 or older (21+ in certain territories). Check your local rules before signing up.
